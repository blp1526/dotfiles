see [this](http://inforati.jp/apple/mac-tips-techniques/system-hints/how-to-use-move-to-folder-command-in-a-open-or-save-dialog-in-macos.html).

* Finder => Preferences
  * Uncheck all checkboxes at "Show these items on the desktop:"
